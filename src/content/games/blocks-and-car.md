Blocks and Car is a fun and casual puzzle game that combines Tetris-style block mechanics with passenger matching gameplay. Different shaped blocks appear on the board, and you clear them just like in classic Tetris. Once blocks are removed, passengers of matching colors are generated and move to the waiting area. Your goal is to guide them to the correct buses and keep everything organized. With simple controls and strategic challenges, this game is perfect for players of all ages!
