Cars vs Zombies is an arcade vehicular survival action game. Drive your car, annihilate waves of zombies, and destroy the interactive environment in the arenas. Earn resources to upgrade your vehicles in the garage: improve stats, install attachable armor, and equip boosters. Multiple maps and vehicle types are available.
