Staris Races For Two Players is a fast-paced 1v1 hyper-casual game where two players race against each other to be the first to reach the top of their staircase.* Each player controls a colored stickman character (Blue for Player 1, Red for Player 2). The goal is simple: collect blocks of your own color scattered around the level, carry them to your staircase, and use them to build or paint the steps. The more blocks you collect, the higher you can climb. The first player to complete their staircase and reach the finish platform wins the match. But be careful &mdash; speed and strategy matter.
